About The Club

The AeC PowerSkills Club (Arts and Crafts Power Skills), founded in 2023, is a dynamic student initiative within the National Higher School of Arts and Crafts of Casablanca (ENSAM Casablanca). Our club stands out for its dual objective: to develop both the Soft Skills and Digital Skills of future Arts and Crafts engineers.

  • Soft Skills Development
  • Digital Skills Enhancement
  • Build a Community
  • Foster Relationships and Friendships
